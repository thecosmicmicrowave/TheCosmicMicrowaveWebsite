🌌 Cosmic Microwave Sponsorship Program

Support our mission to inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators.

🌟 Why Sponsor Us

We are a non-profit 501(c)(3) organization, and all sponsorships are 100% tax-deductible.

By sponsoring our team, you are directly investing in the next generation of engineers. Your support helps us remain committed to making STEM education accessible to all, while expanding our outreach and building innovative, world-class robots.

We charge zero membership fees so that any motivated student can join, regardless of family income. That means we rely on sponsors like you and grants to cover robot parts, event travel, tools, outreach supplies, and registration fees—especially as we prepare to compete at the FTC World Championship in Texas this season.

Advertise your brand while supporting a mission that inspires students to innovate, lead, and build. In return for backing our team, your logo can be featured on team apparel, social media, our robot, and more.
