About The Cosmic Microwave

A rookie team name, but a veteran crew. We bring experience, passion, and a winning mindset to every match.

FTC Team 35817 — The Cosmic Microwave — is officially a rookie team, but our core members are seasoned competitors. We previously made our mark as FTC 27393 "The FBI - FIRST Bot Inventors", where we built an impressive legacy of excellence at North Tapps Middle School.

Our journey includes being Finalist Alliance Captain and winning the Control 2 award at the Cowtown Premier Event, ranking 4th at the Washington State Championship, ranking 4th and winning the Think 2 award at the Asimov Super Qualifier, winning the Control 1 award at the Wu League Tournament, and taking home the Inspire 2 award at the Wu Interleague.
