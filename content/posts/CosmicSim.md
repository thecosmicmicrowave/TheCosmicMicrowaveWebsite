We’re thrilled to announce our new robot design for the 2026–2027 season. After months of prototyping, testing, and late-night code sessions, we’ve settled on a **swerve drive** chassis that gives us unmatched mobility on the field.

**Key features**:

- **Innovative intake system** – quickly grabs and scores game elements.
- **Advanced vision processing** – uses a webcam and TensorFlow for object detection.

We can’t wait to compete this season and show what The Cosmic Microwave is made of.
